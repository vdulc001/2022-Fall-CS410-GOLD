Problem Statement

There exists no site that allows a user to search for a book based on its type (physical or electronic), edition (hardcover or paperback), and condition (new or used) as well as display its cheapest total cost.

Problem Characteristics

* Decrease in physical bookstores and increase in online bookstores * Not all book retailers list the same criteria * Type, edition, condition, available for renting, etc. * Hard to find the lowest overall cost when buying books online * Booksellers often tack on additional fees that balloon the total price * General increase in shipping and handling costs due to COVID 19
