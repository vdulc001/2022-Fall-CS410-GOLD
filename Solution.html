The Solution

Solution Statement

Booked is a web-based application that shows users where to purchase a book based on its type, edition, condition, and lowest total cost.

Solution Characteristics
  • Booked will save time and money during the book-searching process by laying out the type, edition, condition, and cost for the customer
  • Will be done by database searching and web scraping
  • For those who prefer to shop online, they are presented with a link to seller’s website
  • Those that prefer in-person shopping will be presented with bookstore’s address
  • Will introduce user’s to local, independent businesses
